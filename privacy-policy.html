Privacy Policy - Ufo Problems

Last updated: May 2026

Thank you for playing Ufo Problems. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected and used when using the game.

Information Collection

Ufo Problems does not require users to create accounts and does not directly collect personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or home addresses.

However, the game may use third-party services such as advertising providers that can automatically collect limited technical information.

Advertising

Ufo Problems uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

AdMob may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • Approximate location (depending on device settings)
  • App interaction data

This information may be used to provide and improve advertising services.

You can learn more about how Google handles data here:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Children's Privacy

Ufo Problems does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

We value your privacy and strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect any information handled through third-party services.
